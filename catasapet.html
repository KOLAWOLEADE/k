Cat as a pet

Cat is a domestic animal of a small carnivorous mammals

A cat can either be a house cat, a farm cat or a feral cat, the latter ranges freely and avoid human contact.
A domestic cat is valued by humans for companioship and their ability to kill radents.
There are about sixty breeds of cats regconized by various catregistires.
Scientific name of a cat is felis catus
Life span of a cat is between twelve(12) to eighteen(18) years.
The male name for a cat is Tom
The female name for a cat is Queen

charateristics of cats

  • Cat has a strong flexible body, quick reflexes, sharp teeth and retractable claws adapted to killing small prey. It's night vision and sense of smell are well developed.
  • Cat communication includes vocalization like meowing, purring,trilling,hissing,growlinig and grounting.
  • Cat can hear that sound too faint or too high in frequency for human ears, such as those made by mice and others small mammals.
